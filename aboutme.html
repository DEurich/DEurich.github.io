About Me

I first started learning HTML and CSS in middle school when Myspace was the biggest thing. Fast forward, and I’m now the father of a 2-year-old, a 911 EMT, and currently a tower climber building 5G cell towers. Unfortunately, none of these career paths would support my family how I would have liked. I knew I needed something better! It was then that I stumbled across freecodecamp. I fell right back in love with coding, specifically JavaScript. I’ve spent all my free time since learning, reading, coding and I can’t stop.
